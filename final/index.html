About

Quinn Field is a designer and artist living in Boston, MA. Born in the hills of Vermont, he moved to the city to study design. He currently studies graphic design at Massachuessetts College of Art and Design. His passions include creating mobile art, taking photos, and dreaming about his next creation in art. He hopes his sense of design can be used to improve the world around him and fight social justice.
